- When writing use case scenarios describe a goal that an actor can accomplish in a single encounter 
and stay focused on the user's intention, what they really want to accomplish.

Active voice: Omit needless words
For example: 
Astronaut inserts meal package.
System sends pager message.

Use case Prompts
* Who performs System administration tasks?
* Who manages users and security?
* What happens if the system fails?
* Is anyone looking at performance metrics or logs?
